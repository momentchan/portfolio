Serenity Beyond explores a calm, dreamlike landscape where endless fields of flowers move across
borderless hills, immersing the viewer in the quiet strength of nature. This scene evokes peace and
connection, yet subtle abstract objects interrupt the harmony—hinting that the space exists beyond
reality.

By balancing the familiar with the surreal, the work creates a gentle tension that invites
reflection. It becomes an experience that is both soothing and thought-provoking, where natural
beauty merges seamlessly with imaginative possibility.
